About Us

Manalo Autos is an independent vehicle dealership, service centre, collision repair centre and a provider of rental cars as well. We aim to be a one stop shop for all automotive needs. We are a family owned business that is operating in Downtown Winnipeg since 1975. Come visit us at 515 Portage Avenue

The secret to our company's success is our ability to provide excellent customer service and our ability to provide you what you need. The service department is certified to do all kinds of repairs and diagnostics. The collision centre also provides rental cars while your vehicle is being repaired.
